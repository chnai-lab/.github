# CHNAI LAB

CHNAI LAB is a Cambodian student-run product studio. We build and own what we
make, with the goal of giving students real product responsibility, operating
experience, and equity instead of only class-project practice.

Most active product repositories are private because they are future startup
assets. Public work focuses on product positioning, case studies, claim-safe
documentation, and selected demos.

## Product Tracks

- **BayonHub** — opportunity infrastructure for Cambodian tech students looking
  for internships, freelance work, jobs, and portfolio-building paths.
- **Svaeng Yul** — Khmer self-study video learning for AI engineering, data
  science, cybersecurity, software engineering, and related tech fields.
- **Chomkar** — B2B agriculture coordination between buyers, farmers, and
  cooperatives before harvest demand is locked in.
- **Sat Digital** — cyber guard tooling for Telegram communities, groups, and
  websites.
- **Vantrex** — trading software for signals, model-assisted tooling, and
  indicator workflows.
- **PHSAROS** — SME operating system for restaurants, coffee shops, marts,
  staff, inventory, and finance.

## How We Work

- Build narrow, working products before scaling claims.
- Keep user data, source code, credentials, and strategy private by default.
- Use AI agents for speed, review, research, and engineering leverage.
- Document what is shipped, what is simulated, and what still needs validation.
- Treat students as builders with ownership, accountability, and real operating
  discipline.
